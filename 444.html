Wait! What is the Four4Four Event - exactly?

When is it?

Every Thursday for four weeks in February. Each one-hour session will begin at 18:00 in four time zones, so no matter where you are, you can join in. Mark your calendar and get ready to improve your English in a fun, real-world way!

Who will be there?

Four4Four is all about quality interaction. There are only 4 people per group, ensuring a focused and supportive environment. It's a chance to improve your English in small, friendly groups with learners from your part of the world.

What will we do?

Each session is focused on interactive, conversation-based learning. We'll use a variety of topics connected to local and global events and your experiences and interests. This allows you to practice English in natural, real-world situations.

How much is it?

It is only £40 for the entire Four4Four event. Payment can be made using Stripe or International Bank Transfer. Payment can be made in British pounds or your local currency. Please check exchange rates before you pay.
