About Gari

I am a native English teacher and writer with over thirty years of teaching English to students and teachers. I started teaching English to natives with low English levels. This unusual start has given me a unique insight into how a language is learnt and taught. As well as teaching, I have written books and plays. This experience has shown me the importance of English and how it can be used with skill and creativity.
