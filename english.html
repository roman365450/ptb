Basic Grammar

Present Simple Tense

Used for habitual actions and general truths.

✓ I speak English every day.
✓ She works in a hospital.
✓ They live in London.

Present Continuous Tense

Used for actions happening now or temporary situations.

✓ I am learning English right now.
✓ She is reading a book.
✓ They are studying for the exam.

Articles: A, An, The

A/An: Used with singular countable nouns (first mention)
The: Used with specific nouns (already mentioned or unique)

✓ I saw a cat. The cat was black.
✓ She is an engineer.
✓ The sun rises in the east.
