About Past Tense of Banana

Mission statement?

Our mission is to help you master English. It's that simple! To do this, we offer you a learning experience that best suits you as an individual in this age of modern technology.

Who is Paste Tense of Banana for?

Whether you are a student starting your journey, a fellow teacher looking for a fresh perspective, or someone simply curious about the quirks of British culture, Past Tense of Banana is for you. We provide the structure needed to navigate the 21st-century digital landscape, focusing on high expectations rather than restrictive levels. Join us to experience a more authentic, sophisticated, and curiosity-driven way to master the English language.

Our approach

The era of linear, unit-by-unit English instruction is over. In a world of streaming and gaming, learners are redundant. Non-natives can be exposed to English as much as natives, so they are able to learn the same way as natives. Past Tense of Banana works within this new reality. We reject the notion of English levels and define English understanding in terms of "skill" and "experience". This means we teach "advanced" structures like the third conditional and future perfect continuous to everyone from day one. By mirroring how native speakers naturally acquire language - exposure without immediate pressure for perfection - we align our teaching with how the modern world actually communicates.
