OUR FIXED SESSIONS AVAILABLE ON OUR PLATFORM

These sessions are designed for school-aged students in small groups, using dynamic teaching methods and personalized approaches. Once enrolled, the session structure is yours for life. You'll also have access to a supportive community where you can share progress, ask questions, and participate in regular online meetings organized by our academy. Each session leads to a certificate.

Choose the session structure that best suits your child's level and goals, then enroll to begin their learning journey.

THE FIXED SESSION APPROACH

This is our most comprehensive session structure. That is to say, students will experience all aspects of English grammar, vocabulary, pronunciation, with a huge number of exercises, lots of different learning approaches, and of course our teachers guiding everything in the sessions.

Small Groups

We work with school-aged students in groups of no more than four students for a minimum period of ten weeks.

Personalized Learning

Students are placed in groups according to age and level. However, all students are taught all aspects of grammar. Our sessions don't follow a pre-determined pathway of learning.

Dynamic Materials

We don't use books. Instead, we use the experiences and interests of our students as the foundation. Our teachers use AI, modular, and online activities to teach in a dynamic and engaging way.

Online Portfolio

Students are encouraged to keep an online portfolio, which allows them to see their progress and serves as a revision tool. Parents can have access to their child's portfolio.
