About the Fixed Session

We work with school-aged students in groups of no more than four students for a minimum period of ten weeks.

Students are placed in groups according to age and level. However, all students are taught all aspects of grammar. Our sessions don't follow a pre-determined pathway of learning. Students are encouraged to practice using vocabulary and all forms of grammar regardless of age and level. This gives students the maximum amount of time and opportunities to recognise and develop aspects of English they find challenging.

We don't use books. Instead, we use the experiences and interests of our students as the foundation of our teaching material. Our teachers use AI, modular, and online activities to teach aspects of English in a dynamic and engaging way.

In addition, students are encouraged to keep an online portfolio, which will allow students to see their progress and be a revision tool. Parents can have access to their child's portfolio.
