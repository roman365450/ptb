What makes us stand out from other English language centres?

The era of linear, unit-by-unit English instruction is over. In a world of streaming and gaming, learners are constantly exposed to complex language that traditional "beginner-to-advanced" models ignore. Past Tense of Banana rejects these outdated labels, teaching "advanced" structures like the third conditional and future perfect continuous to everyone from day one. By mirroring how native speakers naturally acquire language—exposure without immediate pressure for perfection—we align our teaching with how the modern world actually communicates.
